Common Questions

Wardrobe Assembly FAQ

How much does wardrobe assembly cost in NYC?

Pricing depends on size and complexity. A simple two-door freestanding wardrobe starts around $100–$150, while a multi-frame PAX system with sliding doors, interior organizers, and lighting can range from $250–$600. We provide a flat-rate quote before work begins.

Do you anchor wardrobes to the wall?

Absolutely — and we always do. Tall wardrobes are a tip-over risk, especially in homes with children. We anchor every wardrobe to the wall using the included restraints or our own heavy-duty brackets, at no extra charge.

Can you assemble PAX wardrobes with custom interiors?

Yes. We build complete PAX systems including KOMPLEMENT interior fittings — pull-out trays, shoe shelves, jewelry inserts, trouser hangers, soft-close hinges, and mirror doors. We've assembled thousands of PAX units across NYC.

Do you install closet organizer systems?

Yes. Beyond freestanding wardrobes, we install wall-mounted closet organizer systems from IKEA (BOAXEL), Elfa, ClosetMaid, and other brands. Includes anchoring into studs, leveling, and accessory installation.
