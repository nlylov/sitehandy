Common Questions

Murphy Bed FAQ

How much does Murphy bed installation cost in NYC?

Pricing depends on bed type, size, and whether it includes side cabinets or a desk. A basic vertical twin Murphy bed starts around $350–$500. Full system installs with cabinets can run $900–$1,500. Use our instant calculator above for a quick estimate.

Do you need to anchor a Murphy bed to the wall?

Yes. Murphy beds must be securely anchored to wall studs for safe operation. We locate studs, use lag bolts, and test the mechanism with full load before leaving.

Can you install a Murphy bed in an apartment?

Absolutely. Murphy beds are designed for small spaces, and most NYC apartments are ideal candidates. We work in studios, one-bedrooms, and co-ops — and always coordinate building access if needed.

What brands of Murphy beds do you install?

We install Lori Wall Beds, Bestar, Night & Day Furniture, Wilding Wallbeds, Atlantic Furniture, IKEA hacks, and custom wall bed kits. If it folds into a wall, we build it.
