Common Questions

IKEA Assembly FAQ

How much does IKEA furniture assembly cost in NYC?

Prices depend on the specific item and size. Simple pieces like a KALLAX 1×4 shelf start around $80–$120, while complex systems like a PAX wardrobe with sliding doors can range from $250–$550. Use our instant calculator above for a quick estimate, and we'll confirm the exact flat-rate price before any work begins.

Do you assemble all IKEA product lines?

Yes — we assemble every IKEA series including PAX, KALLAX, MALM, HEMNES, BESTÅ, BILLY, NORDLI, HAVSTA, IDANÄS, and discontinued lines. If it comes in a flat-pack box, we build it.

Can you anchor IKEA furniture to the wall?

Absolutely — and we always do. IKEA's tip-over restraints are included with every assembly at no extra charge. For PAX wardrobes, BILLY bookcases, and tall dressers, wall anchoring isn't optional — it's essential for safety, especially in homes with children.

What if IKEA shipped missing or damaged parts?

We carry common IKEA hardware (cam locks, dowels, screws) and can often complete the assembly on the spot. For manufacturer-specific parts like drawer rails or hinges, we'll help you request replacements through IKEA and return to finish the job at no additional trip charge.

How long does IKEA assembly take?

It depends on the item. A KALLAX shelf or MALM nightstand takes about 30–60 minutes. A PAX wardrobe system with interiors typically takes 2–4 hours. Multi-item orders (e.g., bedroom set) may take a full day. We'll give you a time estimate with your quote.

Do you bring your own tools?

Yes — our technicians arrive fully equipped with professional cordless drills, impact drivers, levels, stud finders, and spare IKEA-specific hardware. You don't need to provide anything.

Can you assemble already-opened or partially built IKEA furniture?

Yes. We can pick up where you left off, or disassemble incorrectly built pieces and start fresh. We've rescued many half-finished PAX and MALM projects.

Do you remove all the packaging?

Yes — packaging removal is included with every assembly at no extra charge. We break down all cardboard, styrofoam, and plastic and take it with us or place it in your building's recycling area.

Can you assemble IKEA kitchen cabinets?

Yes — we assemble IKEA METOD/SEKTION kitchen cabinet systems including base cabinets, wall cabinets, and tall units. For full kitchen installations involving plumbing or electrical connections, we'll discuss scope and provide a separate estimate.

Do you service all NYC boroughs?

Our primary service area covers Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, and Staten Island. For other areas, availability depends on schedule and project scope — contact us to check.
