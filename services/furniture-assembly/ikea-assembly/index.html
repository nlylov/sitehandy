Common Questions

IKEA Assembly FAQ

How much does IKEA furniture assembly cost in NYC?

Prices depend on the specific item and size. Simple pieces like a KALLAX 1×4 shelf start around $80–$120, while complex systems like a PAX wardrobe with sliding doors can range from $250–$550. Use our instant calculator above for a quick estimate, and we'll confirm the exact flat-rate price before any work begins.

Do you assemble all IKEA product lines?

Yes — we assemble every IKEA series including PAX, KALLAX, MALM, HEMNES, BESTÅ, BILLY, NORDLI, HAVSTA, IDANÄS, and discontinued lines. If it comes in a flat-pack box, we build it.

Can you anchor IKEA furniture to the wall?

Absolutely — and we always do. IKEA's tip-over restraints are included with every assembly at no extra charge. For PAX wardrobes, BILLY bookcases, and tall dressers, wall anchoring isn't optional — it's essential for safety, especially in homes with children.

What if IKEA shipped missing or damaged parts?

We carry common IKEA hardware (cam locks, dowels, screws) and can often complete the assembly on the spot. For manufacturer-specific parts like drawer rails or hinges, we'll help you request replacements through IKEA and return to finish the job at no additional trip charge.
