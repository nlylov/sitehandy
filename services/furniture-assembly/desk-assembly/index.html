Desk Assembly FAQ

How much does desk assembly cost in NYC?

A simple writing desk starts around $60–$100. Standing desks with electric motors run $130–$300. Use our instant calculator above for a quick estimate.

Do you assemble standing desks?

Yes. We assemble manual-crank and electric standing desks from Uplift, FlexiSpot, Autonomous, IKEA BEKANT/RODULF, and every other brand. We calibrate the motor, test presets, and make sure everything works perfectly.

Can you assemble an L-shaped desk?

Absolutely. L-shaped and corner desks are one of our most common requests. We ensure proper alignment, all bolts are torqued correctly, and everything is level.

What brands of desks do you assemble?

Any brand — IKEA, Wayfair, Amazon, West Elm, Pottery Barn, Autonomous, Uplift, and more. If it comes in a box, we build it.
