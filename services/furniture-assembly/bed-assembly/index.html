Common Questions

Bed Assembly FAQ

How much does bed frame assembly cost in NYC?

Pricing depends on the type and complexity. Simple platform frames start around $80–$120, while storage beds with hydraulic lifts or bunk beds can range from $150–$350. We always provide a flat-rate quote before work begins — no hourly billing.

Can you assemble any brand of bed frame?

Yes — we assemble bed frames from IKEA, Wayfair, West Elm, CB2, Zinus, Casper, Thuma, Floyd, Article, Amazon, and any other brand. If it comes in a box, we build it.

Do you attach the headboard to the wall?

Absolutely. If your bed frame or headboard requires wall mounting for stability, we'll secure it with the appropriate hardware. Wall-mounted headboards are anchored into studs whenever possible.

Can you assemble bunk beds and loft beds safely?

Yes. Bunk beds and loft beds require precise assembly for structural safety. We follow manufacturer guidelines exactly, ensure all guardrails are secure, and verify stability before we leave.
