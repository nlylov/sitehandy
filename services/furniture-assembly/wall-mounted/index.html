Common Questions

Wall-Mounted Furniture FAQ

How much does wall-mounted furniture installation cost in NYC?

Pricing depends on the item and wall type. Floating shelves start around $60–$100 per unit, while wall-mounted entertainment centers or large cabinet systems can range from $150–$400. We always provide a flat-rate quote before starting.

Can you mount furniture on any wall type?

Yes. We work with drywall, plaster, brick, concrete, and tile walls. Each wall type requires different anchoring methods — toggle bolts for drywall, masonry anchors for brick and concrete. We assess your wall and use the correct hardware for a secure hold.

Do you find studs before mounting?

Always. We use professional stud finders to locate wall studs and mount directly into them whenever possible. For items that can't align with studs, we use heavy-duty wall anchors rated for the weight of your furniture.

What wall-mounted items can you install?

We install floating shelves, wall cabinets, entertainment centers and media consoles, kitchen wall organizers, bathroom vanity cabinets, IKEA EKET and BESTÅ wall units, and any other wall-mounted furniture or storage system.
