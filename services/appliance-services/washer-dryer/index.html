Common Questions

Washer & Dryer FAQ

How much does installation cost?

Flat-rate pricing based on the type of setup. We provide a transparent quote before any work begins.

Can you stack my washer and dryer?

Yes. We install stacking kits for compatible front-load units and verify weight ratings, anti-vibration pads, and leveling.

Do you connect water and drain lines?

Yes. We connect hot and cold supply hoses, verify drain standpipe fit, and test for leaks.

Can you haul away old machines?

We can disconnect and move old units. Hauling and disposal can be arranged upon request.
