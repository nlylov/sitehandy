Need a washer installed in New York City? Repair Asap LLC installs washing machines across Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, and Staten Island. We handle top-load, front-load, and stackable washers from Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, GE, Maytag, and every other brand.

Common Questions

Washer Installation FAQ

How much does washer installation cost in NYC?

Standard washer installation runs $120–$200 including hookup and testing. Stackable units may be higher.

Do you connect water supply lines?

Yes. We connect hot and cold supply hoses, the drain hose, and test for leaks at all connection points.

Can you install a stackable washer?

Absolutely. We install stackable washer/dryer combos, including the stacking kit.

Do you remove the old washer?

We can disconnect and move your old unit. Haul-away can be arranged for an additional fee.
