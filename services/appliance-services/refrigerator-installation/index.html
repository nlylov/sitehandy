Common Questions

Refrigerator FAQ

How much does installation cost?

Flat-rate pricing. Simple slide-in setups are most affordable. Water line and door reversal are priced as add-ons.

Can you connect the ice maker water line?

Yes. We tap into the nearest cold water supply and run braided stainless steel line.

Do you reverse refrigerator doors?

Yes. We reverse door hinges on compatible models for your kitchen layout.

Can you remove the old fridge?

Yes. We disconnect and move the old unit. Hauling can be arranged.
