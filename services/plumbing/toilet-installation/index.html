Need a toilet replaced or repaired in your NYC apartment? Repair Asap LLC installs standard, elongated, comfort-height, dual-flush, and wall-hung toilets across Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, and Staten Island.

Every install includes removing the old unit, new wax ring, fresh closet bolts, supply line connection, caulking at the base, and multi-flush leak testing. We also repair running toilets, replace fill valves, and install bidet seats. Flat-rate pricing, 1-year workmanship warranty.

Common Questions

Toilet FAQ

What types do you install?

Standard, elongated, comfort-height, dual-flush, wall-hung, bidet seats.

Do you remove the old toilet?

Yes. Disconnect, remove, dispose. New wax ring and bolts included.

Can you fix a running toilet?

Yes. Fill valve, flapper, flush valve, handle repairs.

How much does it cost?

Flat-rate including removal, install, and leak testing.
