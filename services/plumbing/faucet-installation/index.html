Upgrading your kitchen faucet or replacing a leaky bathroom tap? Repair Asap LLC installs faucets of all types across Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, and Staten Island. Pull-down sprayers, single-hole, widespread, and wall-mount configurations.

Every install includes removing the old faucet, cleaning the mounting area, installing the new unit, connecting water lines, and leak testing. Flat-rate pricing, 1-year workmanship warranty.

Common Questions

Faucet FAQ

What faucets do you install?

Kitchen, bathroom, bathtub, laundry, bar. All mounting styles.

Do you supply the faucet?

You provide it or we source it. We confirm product and cost first.

How long does it take?

Standard swap: 1–2 hours. Longer if plumbing needs adapting.

How much does it cost?

Flat-rate per faucet including removal, install, and leak test.
