Common Questions

Projector Mounting FAQ

How much does projector mounting cost in NYC?

Pricing depends on mount type (ceiling vs. wall), cable routing requirements, and ceiling material. Use our instant calculator above for a quick estimate — we confirm the flat-rate price before any work begins.

Can you mount a projector on a concrete ceiling?

Yes. We use masonry drill bits and concrete anchors rated for the projector's weight. We've mounted projectors on concrete, drywall, and drop-ceiling grids across NYC apartments and offices.

Do you run cables for the projector?

Yes. We route HDMI, power, and audio cables through the ceiling or along walls using raceways for a clean, professional look. In-wall cable runs are available for drywall ceilings.

Do you align the projector image after mounting?

Absolutely. We adjust the projector's keystone correction, zoom, focus, and lens shift to ensure a perfectly aligned image on your screen or wall. We test everything before we leave.
