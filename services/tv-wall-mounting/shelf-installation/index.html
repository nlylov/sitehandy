Common Questions

Shelf Mounting FAQ

How much does shelf mounting cost in NYC?

Pricing depends on the number of shelves, shelf type, and wall type. A single floating shelf on drywall starts around $60–$100. Use our instant calculator above for a quick estimate — we'll confirm the exact flat-rate price before any work begins.

Can you mount shelves on brick or concrete walls?

Yes. We use masonry drill bits, tapcon screws, and heavy-duty anchors designed for brick and concrete. Every shelf is leveled and load-tested before we leave.

How much weight can a floating shelf hold?

It depends on the shelf design, mounting hardware, and wall type. Shelves mounted into studs can typically hold 30–50+ lbs per bracket. We assess your wall and recommend the right hardware for what you plan to store.

Do you supply the shelves or just install them?

We install shelves you've already purchased. If you need help choosing, we can recommend options based on your space and weight requirements. We bring all mounting hardware and tools.
