Common Questions

Mirror Mounting FAQ

How do you mount a heavy mirror on drywall?

We locate studs with an electronic stud finder and anchor directly into them when possible. For mirrors between studs, we use heavy-duty toggle bolts or snap toggles rated for the mirror's weight. Every mirror is tested for hold before we leave.

Can you mount a frameless mirror in a bathroom?

Yes. We install frameless mirrors using mirror clips, J-channels, or construction adhesive. We account for bathroom humidity and use moisture-resistant hardware.

How much does mirror mounting cost in NYC?

Pricing depends on mirror size, weight, wall type, and mounting method. A standard bathroom vanity mirror starts around $60–$100. Use our instant calculator above for a quick estimate.

Can you mount a mirror on a brick or tile wall?

Yes. We use masonry drill bits and specialized anchors for brick, and tile-specific bits to prevent cracking. We've mounted mirrors on every surface type found in NYC apartments.
