Common Questions

Art Installation FAQ

How much does art installation cost in NYC?

Single piece hanging runs $40–$80. Gallery walls (5+ pieces) run $150–$400 depending on complexity.

Can you hang heavy art?

Yes. We install French cleats and heavy-duty anchors for pieces up to 100+ lbs.

Do you help plan gallery wall layouts?

Absolutely. We help with spacing, arrangement, and use paper templates before drilling.

Can you hang art on concrete or brick?

Yes. We use masonry bits and concrete anchors for brick, concrete, and cinder block walls.
