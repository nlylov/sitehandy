Common Questions

TV Mounting FAQ

How much does TV mounting cost in NYC?

Pricing depends on TV size, wall type, and mount style. A standard drywall mount for a 50–55" TV starts around $150–$220. Use our instant calculator above for a quick estimate — we'll confirm the exact flat-rate price before any work begins.

Can you mount a TV on a brick or concrete wall?

Yes. We work with all wall types — drywall, plaster, brick, concrete, stone, and metal studs. We bring masonry drill bits, tapcon screws, and heavy-duty anchors specifically designed for each surface.

Do you hide the cables?

Yes. We offer in-wall cable concealment for drywall installations and surface-mounted cable raceways for brick and concrete walls. Both options give you a clean, wire-free look.

Can you mount a TV above a fireplace?

Absolutely. We've mounted hundreds of TVs above fireplaces across NYC apartments. We use heat-rated mounts, verify clearance requirements, and run cables safely away from heat sources.
