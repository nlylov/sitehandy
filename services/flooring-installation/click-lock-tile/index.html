Common Questions

Click-Lock Tile FAQ

What is click-lock tile?

Interlocking tile panels that snap together without mortar. Look like real stone or porcelain.

Where can it be installed?

Kitchens, bathrooms, entryways, laundry rooms, and basements.

How long does it take?

Faster than traditional tile. Standard bathroom or kitchen in about 1 day.

Can it go over existing tile?

Yes, if the surface is flat and level. We assess subfloor before starting.
