Need laminate flooring installed in your NYC apartment or home? Repair Asap LLC handles click-lock, glue-down, and waterproof laminate from Pergo, Mohawk, Shaw, and every other brand across Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, and Staten Island.

Every installation includes old flooring removal (if needed), subfloor inspection, underlayment, precise cutting around doorways, transition strips, and baseboard reinstallation. Per-square-foot pricing, 1-year workmanship warranty.

Common Questions

Laminate Flooring FAQ

How much does it cost?

Per square foot pricing. Includes underlayment, transitions, and baseboard. Exact quote after measuring.

Do you remove old flooring?

Yes. Carpet, vinyl, or old laminate. Subfloor prep included if needed.

How long does it take?

Standard room: about 1 day. Larger spaces: 2–3 days.

Can it go over concrete?

Yes, with vapor barrier underlayment. We test moisture levels first.
