Common Questions

AC Cleaning FAQ

How much does AC deep cleaning cost?

Pricing depends on the unit type and condition. We offer flat-rate pricing per unit with multi-unit discounts for apartments and hotels.

What does AC deep cleaning include?

Full disassembly, evaporator and condenser coil wash, blower fan cleaning, drain pan flush, mold treatment, filter cleaning or replacement, and reassembly with performance testing.

How often should I deep clean my AC?

At least once a year — ideally before summer. High-humidity or dusty environments may need twice-yearly service.

Can you clean mold out of my AC?

Yes. Mold on coils, drain pans, and blower wheels is common. We use EPA-registered antimicrobial treatments and verify the unit is mold-free before reassembly.
