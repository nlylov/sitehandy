Common Questions

Window AC FAQ

How much does window AC installation cost in NYC?

Pricing depends on the unit size, window type, and whether a support bracket is needed. Standard installations start at a competitive flat rate. We always provide a transparent quote before work begins — no hourly billing.

Do you supply the support bracket?

Yes. NYC building code requires window AC units above the first floor to have exterior support brackets. We supply and install code-compliant brackets rated for your unit's weight.

Can you install a window AC in a casement or slider window?

Yes. We install window ACs in double-hung, casement, slider, and even non-standard windows. Special adapter kits and custom framing are available for tricky openings.

Do you remove and dispose of old AC units?

Absolutely. We safely remove your old unit, cap the window, and can haul it away for proper disposal. Just let us know when you book.
