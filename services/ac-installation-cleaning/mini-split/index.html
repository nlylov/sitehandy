Common Questions

Mini-Split FAQ

How much does mini-split installation cost?

Pricing depends on zones, line-set length, and electrical requirements. We provide a detailed quote after assessment.

Do mini-splits heat and cool?

Yes. Most modern mini-splits are heat pumps providing efficient cooling in summer and heating in winter — often down to -15°F.

Do I need a dedicated electrical circuit?

Yes. Mini-splits require a dedicated 20A or 30A circuit. We can install the circuit as part of the project.

How long does installation take?

Single-zone typically takes 4–6 hours. Multi-zone systems may take 1–2 days depending on the number of indoor heads.
