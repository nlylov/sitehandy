Common Questions

Portable AC FAQ

How much does portable AC setup cost in NYC?

Setup typically includes window venting kit installation, hose routing, and drainage configuration. Pricing is flat-rate and depends on your window type and unit model. We provide a quote before starting.

Do you install the window venting kit?

Yes. We cut and fit the exhaust panel to your window, seal gaps with weatherstripping, and ensure the hose is properly connected for maximum efficiency.

Can you set up a portable AC with no window?

In most cases a window or sliding door is needed for venting. However, we can route exhaust through drop ceilings, dryer vents, or custom wall ports where building conditions allow.

Do portable ACs need drainage?

Many modern units are self-evaporative, but some require a drain hose or tank emptying. We configure your unit's drainage — continuous drain hose, gravity drain, or condensate pump — based on its design.
