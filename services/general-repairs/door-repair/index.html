Common Questions

Door Repair FAQ

What problems do you fix?

Sticking, sagging, won't latch, squeaky hinges, damaged frames, holes, and lock issues.

Do you replace doors?

Yes. Pre-hung and slab doors. Measuring, trimming, hinge mortising, and hardware included.

Can you fix a door that won't close?

Yes. Usually shifted hinges, warped frame, or strike plate misalignment.

How much does it cost?

Flat-rate by repair type. Simple adjustments are affordable. Full replacements quoted after assessment.
