Need a drywall hole or crack fixed in your NYC apartment? Repair Asap LLC handles nail holes, doorknob holes, fist-size openings, ceiling cracks, and water-damaged drywall across Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, and Staten Island.

Every repair includes cutting, backing, taping, mudding, sanding, texture matching, and priming. The result is a seamless patch you can't tell from the rest of the wall. Flat-rate pricing, 1-year workmanship warranty.

Common Questions

Drywall Patch FAQ

How much does it cost?

Flat-rate per patch. Small to fist-size holes are affordable. Large repairs quoted after assessment.

Can you match the texture?

Yes. Orange peel, knockdown, smooth — we match it seamlessly.

Do you paint it?

We prime the patch. Can paint to match if you provide the paint.

How long does it take?

Small patches: same day. Larger repairs may take 2 visits for proper curing.
