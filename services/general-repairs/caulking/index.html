Common Questions

Caulking FAQ

Where do you apply caulking?

Tubs, showers, sinks, toilets, windows, doors, countertops, baseboards.

What type of caulk?

Mold-resistant silicone for wet areas, paintable latex for trim, polyurethane for exterior.

How long does it last?

5–10 years. We fully remove old caulk and clean before applying new.

How much does it cost?

Flat-rate by area. Standard tub re-caulk is very affordable.
