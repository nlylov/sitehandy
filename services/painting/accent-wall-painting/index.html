Need an accent wall painted in New York City? Repair Asap LLC paints accent walls and feature walls across Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, and Staten Island — solid colors, two-tone designs, and geometric patterns.

Every accent wall job includes surface prep, taping, priming (if needed), two coats of premium paint, and a clean edge finish. We help with color selection and can create custom patterns.

Common Questions

Accent Wall Painting FAQ

How much does accent wall painting cost in NYC?

A single accent wall typically runs $150–$350 depending on wall size and patterns involved.

Do you supply the paint?

We can use paint you provide or source premium paint (Benjamin Moore, Sherwin-Williams).

Can you paint geometric patterns?

Yes. We create stripes, chevrons, color blocks, and custom geometric designs with precise taping.

How long does it take?

A single accent wall typically takes 3–5 hours including prep, two coats, and cleanup.
