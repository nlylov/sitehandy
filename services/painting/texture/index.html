Common Questions

Texture FAQ

What is a skim coat?

A thin layer of joint compound for a smooth, flat finish. Covers old texture and imperfections.

Can you remove popcorn ceilings?

Yes. Wet-scrape, skim coat, sand, prime, paint.

What textures do you offer?

Smooth, orange peel, knockdown, skip trowel, and texture matching for patches.

How much does it cost?

Per-square-foot pricing. Quoted after assessing wall condition.
