Need a room painted in your NYC apartment? Repair Asap LLC provides professional interior painting across Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, and Staten Island. Single rooms, accent walls, hallways, ceilings, and full apartment repaints.

Every job includes wall prep (holes patched, sanded, primed), 2 coats of premium low-VOC paint, ceiling and trim, furniture protection, and cleanup. Per-room pricing, 1-year workmanship warranty.

Common Questions

Interior Painting FAQ

How much does it cost?

Per-room pricing. Includes prep, 2 coats, ceiling, trim, cleanup. Quoted after measuring.

Do you move furniture?

Yes. We move, cover, and protect everything. Floors get drop cloths.

What paint do you use?

Benjamin Moore, Sherwin-Williams, or your preferred brand. Low-VOC, premium-grade.

How long does it take?

Standard room: 1 day. Full apartment: 2–4 days.
