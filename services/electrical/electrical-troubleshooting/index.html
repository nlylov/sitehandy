Common Questions

Troubleshooting FAQ

How much does troubleshooting cost?

Flat diagnostic fee to find the problem. Repairs quoted separately after diagnosis.

Why do my breakers keep tripping?

Overloaded circuits, short circuits, ground faults, or failing breakers. We fix the root cause.

Why are my lights flickering?

Loose wiring, failing switch, voltage fluctuation, or overloaded circuit. We diagnose systematically.

Is a buzzing outlet dangerous?

Yes — buzzing can indicate arcing or loose connections, both fire hazards. Call us for same-day inspection.
